Agriculture with integrity.

Akimo Group's Farms division unites Ego Animal Farm and Onyinye Palm Oil Farm under one operating philosophy: produce more while respecting the land. Our livestock, feed, and palm operations are guided by veterinary oversight, zero-waste targets, and community development programs.

Ego Farm Ltd

Ego Animal Farm Ltd is a modern integrated livestock production facility focused on sustainable animal husbandry and the production of high-quality protein sources. Our operations encompass large-scale poultry farming with climate-controlled houses, dairy farming featuring Holstein and local breeds, cattle ranching, and fish farming using advanced aquaculture techniques. We maintain strict biosecurity protocols, employ veterinary professionals, and utilize modern feeding systems to ensure optimal animal health and productivity while meeting the growing demand for fresh, organic, and traceable animal products across Nigeria.

Onyinye Palm Oil Farm Ltd

Onyinye Palm Oil Farm Ltd is a leading agricultural enterprise specializing in sustainable palm oil cultivation, processing, and export. Our expansive plantations utilize high-yielding oil palm varieties and implement best agricultural practices including integrated pest management, precision farming techniques, and environmentally sustainable harvesting methods. We operate modern palm oil mills equipped with advanced extraction and refining technology to produce premium grade crude palm oil, refined palm oil, and palm kernel products for both domestic consumption and international export markets, particularly to Europe and Asia.
